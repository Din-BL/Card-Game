Welcome to the War Battle!

In this game, you'll be playing against the computer in a fast-paced battle to see who can earn the most points within 30 seconds. To start the game, simply press the "Create Deck" button. You'll then be able to start battling the cards against the computer. But be careful! You must press the "play" button within 5 seconds!, or the computer will automatically win the round. As you battle the cards, you'll earn points based on the cards you play. At the end of the 30-second time limit, the player with the most points wins the game. Good luck, and have fun!
